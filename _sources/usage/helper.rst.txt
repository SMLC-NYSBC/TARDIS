Conversion
__________

Convert between csv and Amira .am format
`````````````````````````````````````````
TARDIS will help you to convert between csv and am file format. Simply use:

Usage:

.. code-block::

    tardis -dir <path-to-your-tomograms> -f csv_am
    tardis -dir <path-to-your-tomograms> -f am_csv
